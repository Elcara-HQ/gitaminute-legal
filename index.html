Privacy Policy

Information we collect

Account information. Signing in is required to use the app. When you sign in with Google or with an email and password, we collect your email address, and, if you provide one, your display name. Authentication is handled by Supabase, our backend provider.

Content you create. The app stores the content you create — bookmarks, highlights, notes, reflections, reading progress, and settings — so it can be synced across your devices.

AI chat messages. If you use the "Ask AI" feature, the questions you send are processed to generate a response. Please do not include sensitive personal information in chat messages.

We do not collect your precise location or contacts, we do not use advertising trackers, and the app contains no ads.

How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account and sign you in.
  • To sync your saved verses, notes, and reflections across your devices.
  • To provide the AI guide's answers to your questions.
  • To send the daily verse reminder you opt into (handled on your device).

How your information is shared

We share data only with the service providers that make the app work: Supabase (authentication and storage), Google (if you choose "Sign in with Google"), and the AI provider that powers "Ask AI" (receives your chat questions to generate answers). We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention and security

We keep your account data for as long as your account exists. We use industry-standard measures to protect your data in transit and at rest.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Delete Your Account & Data

You can request deletion of your Daily Gita, Bhagavad Gita account and all associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

Email vedantlavale@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line "Delete my account". We will verify the request and delete your account within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and email address
  • Your display name
  • Your saved content — bookmarks, highlights, notes, reflections, and reading progress

Anonymous, aggregated logs that cannot identify you may be retained for security and operational purposes.
